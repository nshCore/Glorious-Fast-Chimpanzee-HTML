FAQ

Common questions

Here are some of the most common questions that we get.
What is an Emotional Support Animal (ESA)?An Emotional Support Animal (ESA) is a companion animal that provides therapeutic benefit to individuals with mental or emotional disabilities.
Do I need a certification for my Emotional Support Animal?While certification is not required by law, having an ESA certification can help in providing legitimacy and access to certain benefits.
How do I qualify for an Emotional Support Animal certification?To qualify for an ESA certification, you must have a diagnosed mental or emotional disability that is recognized in the DSM-5 by a licensed mental health professional.
Is the Emotional Support Animal certification valid nationwide?Yes, the Emotional Support Animal certification obtained through our website is valid nationwide and can be used to access housing and travel accommodations.
Can any animal be registered as an Emotional Support Animal?While most domesticated animals can be registered as ESAs, it is recommended to choose an animal that provides you with the most comfort and support for your emotional needs.
